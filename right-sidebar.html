Summary

This is a collection of 3D models I have created, unwrapped, and textured. All models were created in 3DS Max. The ones on display are a mix of models created for the game Ichor Burns and models created for my personal project "Nature Wars".

The wooden crate and metal shed were prop assets for the level design of Ichor Burns, only the crate made its way into the final game after an art style change due to the short deadline. Each model was made to be low poly to keep in line with other props and to not impact the performance of the game. The other models are weapon assents for my personal project "Nature Wars", and low poly and have simple textures to keep in line with the art style of the game.
